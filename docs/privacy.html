O'zbek

KiSA ("biz", "ilova") shaxsiy moliyaviy boshqaruv ilovasidir. Ushbu siyosat KiSA qanday ma'lumotlarni yig'ishi, ulardan qanday foydalanishi va ularni qanday himoya qilishini tushuntiradi.

1. Biz yig'adigan ma'lumotlar

Biz hech qanday shaxsiy ma'lumot yig'maymiz. KiSA — to'liq offline ilova. Siz kiritgan barcha ma'lumotlar (kirim/chiqimlar, hamyon balanslari, maqsadlar, izohlar va sozlamalar) faqat sizning qurilmangizda saqlanadi va hech qayerga yuborilmaydi. Ilova ro'yxatdan o'tish, email yoki akkaunt talab qilmaydi.

Biz joylashuv, kontaktlar, reklama identifikatorlari yoki kuzatuv (tracking) ma'lumotlarini yig'maymiz. Ilovada reklama yo'q.

2. Ma'lumotlaringiz qayerda saqlanadi

Barcha ma'lumotlar qurilmangiz xotirasida (lokal) saqlanadi va ilova internetsiz to'liq ishlaydi. Ma'lumotlaringiz bulutga yoki bizning serverlarimizga yuborilmaydi.

3. Uchinchi tomon xizmatlari

Ilova faqat bitta tashqi so'rov yuboradi: dollar kursini ko'rsatish uchun O'zbekiston Markaziy banki (cbu.uz) ochiq ma'lumotlarini oladi. Bu so'rovga hech qanday shaxsiy ma'lumot yuborilmaydi.

4. Ma'lumotlarni o'chirish

Istalgan vaqtda barcha ma'lumotlaringizni o'chirishingiz mumkin: Sozlamalar → Barcha ma'lumotlarni o'chirish. Yoki ilovani qurilmangizdan o'chiring — shunda hamma narsa butunlay o'chadi.

5. Bolalar

KiSA 13 yoshdan kichik bolalar uchun mo'ljallanmagan va ulardan ataylab ma'lumot yig'maydi.

6. O'zgarishlar

Ushbu siyosatni vaqti-vaqti bilan yangilashimiz mumkin. Yangilanish sanasini sahifa tepasida ko'rishingiz mumkin.

English

KiSA ("we", "the app") is a personal finance management application. This policy explains what information KiSA collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect any personal data. KiSA is a fully offline app. Everything you enter (income/expenses, wallet balances, goals, notes and settings) is stored only on your device and is never sent anywhere. The app requires no registration, email or account.

We do not collect location, contacts, advertising identifiers or tracking data. The app contains no ads.

2. Where your data is stored

All data is stored locally in your device's storage, and the app works fully offline. Your data is never sent to the cloud or to our servers.

3. Third-party services

The app makes only one external request: it fetches public data from the Central Bank of Uzbekistan (cbu.uz) to display the USD exchange rate. No personal data is sent with this request.

4. Deleting your data

You can delete all your data at any time: Settings → Clear All Data. Or simply delete the app from your device — everything will be permanently removed.

5. Children

KiSA is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The last updated date is shown at the top of this page.
