O'zbek

KiSA ("biz", "ilova") shaxsiy moliyaviy boshqaruv ilovasidir. Ushbu siyosat KiSA qanday ma'lumotlarni yig'ishi, ulardan qanday foydalanishi va ularni qanday himoya qilishini tushuntiradi.

1. Biz yig'adigan ma'lumotlar

  • Akkaunt ma'lumotlari: agar siz ro'yxatdan o'tsangiz — email manzilingiz va ko'rsatish ismingiz (ixtiyoriy).
  • Moliyaviy ma'lumotlar: siz kiritgan kirim/chiqimlar, hamyon balanslari, maqsadlar va izohlar. Bu ma'lumotlar faqat siz uchun va sizga ko'rsatish maqsadida saqlanadi.
  • Sozlamalar: til, mavzu (qorong'u/yorug'), valyuta kabi afzalliklar.

Biz joylashuv, kontaktlar, reklama identifikatorlari yoki kuzatuv (tracking) ma'lumotlarini yig'maymiz. Ilovada reklama yo'q.

2. Ma'lumotlardan qanday foydalanamiz

Ma'lumotlar faqat ilovaning asosiy vazifasi — moliyangizni ko'rsatish, hisoblash va qurilmalaringiz orasida sinxronlash uchun ishlatiladi. Biz ma'lumotlaringizni sotmaymiz va reklama uchun ishlatmaymiz.

3. Saqlash va sinxronlash

Ma'lumotlaringiz birinchi navbatda qurilmangizda lokal saqlanadi va ilova internetsiz ham ishlaydi. Agar tizimga kirsangiz, ma'lumotlaringiz xavfsiz sinxronlash uchun Google Firebase (Authentication va Cloud Firestore) xizmatlarida saqlanadi.

4. Uchinchi tomon xizmatlari

  • Google Firebase — akkaunt (Authentication) va bulutli saqlash/sinxronlash (Cloud Firestore). Maxfiyligi: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • O'zbekiston Markaziy banki (cbu.uz) — dollar kursini ko'rsatish uchun. Bu so'rovga hech qanday shaxsiy ma'lumot yuborilmaydi.

5. Akkauntni va ma'lumotlarni o'chirish

Istalgan vaqtda ilova ichida akkauntingizni o'chirishingiz mumkin: Sozlamalar → Akkauntni o'chirish. Bu sizning akkauntingizni va barcha ma'lumotlaringizni (lokal va bulutdagi) butunlay o'chiradi. Bu amalni qaytarib bo'lmaydi.

6. Bolalar

KiSA 13 yoshdan kichik bolalar uchun mo'ljallanmagan va ulardan ataylab ma'lumot yig'maydi.

7. O'zgarishlar

English

KiSA ("we", "the app") is a personal finance management application. This policy explains what information KiSA collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: if you register — your email address and display name (optional).
  • Financial data: the income/expenses, wallet balances, goals and notes you enter. This data is stored solely to display it back to you.
  • Preferences: settings such as language, theme and currency.

We do not collect location, contacts, advertising identifiers or tracking data. The app contains no ads.

2. How we use the data

Data is used only for the app's core purpose — to display, calculate and sync your finances across your devices. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

3. Storage and sync

Your data is stored locally on your device first, and the app works fully offline. If you sign in, your data is stored with Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) for secure sync.

4. Third-party services

  • Google Firebase — account (Authentication) and cloud storage/sync (Cloud Firestore). Privacy
  • Central Bank of Uzbekistan (cbu.uz) — to display the USD exchange rate. No personal data is sent with this request.

5. Deleting your account and data

You can delete your account at any time inside the app: Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all your data (local and cloud). This action cannot be undone.

6. Children

KiSA is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The last updated date is shown at the top of this page.
